Formula SAE Tire Modeling

Tire choice and justification is an integral part in vehicle and suspension design. The primary goal of this project is to take data provided by the Formula SAE TTC (Tire Testing Consortium) and Calspan to make a justified tire choice for the 49ers Racing FSAE car. The software uses a simplified Pacejka tire model to fit the provided raw data for comparison of different tire brands and compounds. The project is still growing and more features are being added to provide a more accurate tire model and build a stronger justification for tire choice.
