About Us

The 49ers Racing Vehicle Dynamics Subteam is dedicated to advancing the field of vehicle dynamics through innovative research and practical applications. Our team consists of passionate engineering students who work together to design, test, and optimize a Formula SAE car.

What We Do

Formula SAE is an international collegiate design competition that challenges students to design, build, and drive a Formula style racecar. The Vehicle Dynamics Subteam focuses on the performance of the car, including the suspension and tires. By applying engineering principles students learn how to optimize the car's handling and performance on the track.
