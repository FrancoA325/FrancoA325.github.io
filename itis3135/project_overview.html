Project Overview

Project Overview

Description of the Application: This web application is designed for the 49ers Racing Vehicle Dynamics Subteam to streamline communication, showcase team projects, and provide valuable tools and resources for current and prospective team members. The site will serve as a hub for vehicle dynamics work, offering access to media, design documentation, weekly updates, and engineering tools.

Intended Users of the Website: The website is intended for members of the 49ers Racing Vehicle Dynamics Subteam, other subteams in the 49ers Racing organization, potential recruits, sponsors, and motorsport enthusiasts interested in the technical aspects of vehicle dynamics.

Overview of the Content of the Website:

  • Photos and Media: A gallery of team events, project progress, and vehicle testing.
  • CAD Design and Notes: Documentation and 3D models for the vehicle's design.
  • Weekly Meeting Notes: A log of weekly meeting minutes to track progress and team actions.
  • Calculators and Tools: Online tools for vehicle dynamics calculations, such as tire force, suspension geometry, and weight distribution.

Page Design

  • Page 1: Home
    • Purpose: Introduces the team and provides a gateway to all other pages.
    • Audience: All visitors, including sponsors, recruits, and team members.
    • Content: Introduction to the subteam, mission statement, and navigation links.
    • Interactivity: Interactive welcome banner with dynamic team news.
  • Page 2: Photos and Media
    • Purpose: Displays images and videos related to vehicle design, testing, and team events.
    • Audience: General visitors, sponsors, and team members.
    • Content: Photo galleries with captions, embedded videos from testing sessions.
    • Interactivity: Lightbox-style image gallery for easy viewing.

  • Page 4: Weekly Meeting Notes
    • Purpose: Logs meeting minutes and actions to keep track of weekly progress.
    • Audience: Team members and project leads.

  • Page 5: Calculators and Tools
    • Purpose: Provides tools for vehicle dynamics calculations, such as tire force and suspension geometry.
    • Audience: Engineers and technical team members.

Dynamic Functionality

JavaScript-based interactivity: On the Photos and Media page, a lightbox gallery will be used to allow users to view images in an interactive format. On the CAD Design and Notes page, a 3D viewer for CAD models will let users interact with designs. The Calculators and Tools page will have custom-built calculators for suspension setup, tire force analysis, with interaction using JavaScript.
