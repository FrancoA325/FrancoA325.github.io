Best Tracks

For any NASCAR fan, Charlotte Motor Speedway (CMS) is a must-see. Located in the heart of racing country, CMS hosts some of the biggest events in NASCAR, including the Coca-Cola 600 and the All-Star Race. Its rich history, combined with state-of-the-art facilities, makes it a top destination for fans and drivers alike.

Talladega Superspeedway and Darlington Raceway are also iconic stops on the NASCAR circuit. Talladega is famous for its towering grandstands, blistering speeds, and the adrenaline-pumping unpredictability of pack racing. Meanwhile, Darlington, with its unique, egg-shaped layout, presents a challenge like no other. Known as “The Lady in Black,” Darlington is steeped in tradition and remains one of the most challenging and revered tracks in NASCAR.

Last but not least, Daytona International Speedway, home of the Daytona 500. A legendary venue that embodies the spirit and excitement of NASCAR.

Getting Started: Watching and Local Racing

Getting into NASCAR is a great way to connect with motorsports, whether you're watching or participating. Start by learning the basics, watching major races like the Daytona 500, and following popular drivers. Attending local races at smaller tracks gives you firsthand experience and insight into different racing series like the Xfinity or Truck Series.

For those interested in racing, local leagues offer entry-level classes such as street stock or mini stock, making it easy to start on a budget. These grassroots events are beginner-friendly and provide a community for learning. Getting advice from local racers can help you quickly gain experience in the world of racing.

There are plenty of sites and tools to help you learn the ins and outs of the sport. the NASCAR mobile app provides radio coverage for each of the races as well as live timing and scoring. The NASCAR website also has a wealth of information on the sport and its history. Other sites like jayski.com provide up-to-date news and information on the sport as well as technical information on the cars and tracks.
