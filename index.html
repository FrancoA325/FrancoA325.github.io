Formula SAE Tire Modeling

Tire choice and justification are integral in vehicle and suspension design. This project uses data from Formula SAE TTC (Tire Testing Consortium) and Calspan to justify tire selection for the 49ers Racing FSAE car. A simplified Pacejka tire model fits raw data for comparison of tire brands and compounds, with ongoing improvements for accuracy.
